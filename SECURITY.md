## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please report it by:

1.  Opening a **GitHub Issue** (if it's not sensitive) with the label `security`.
2.  If the issue is sensitive, please email us at `security@example.com` (replace with actual contact if available) or reach out to the maintainer directly.

We plan to acknowledge your report within 48 hours and will send a more detailed response within 96 hours indicating the next steps in handling your report.
